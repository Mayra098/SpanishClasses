About The Course

The classes are aimed at people of all ages and levels, as they are tailored to the student´s individual needs. Throughout the course you will gain knowledge of everything you need to communicate in Spanish: correct use of language, good pronunciation, reading and writing. And not only that: you will also learn about Latin American culture and history.

FAQ

How long are the classes?

iClasses usually last 2 hours, but you can specify the length and the timing of the classes.

Do I need to have any previous knowledge of Spanish to take the course?

No, everyone can learn Spanish, no matter how much you know about the language!

Can the classes be done online?

Yes, if you are not able to take classes in person, the course can be carried out online via videoconference platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Skype, etc.)
